The opening ceremony of the renovation
of the citizens toilet at fish station was held

October 17, 2020(the 2st year of Reiwa) (Saturday) 13:00~
In the middle of the Uonotana Shopping Street the renovation
project of the citizens toilet at fish station has been completed
and the opening ceremony has been held.

The public toilet inside fish station has been renovated.

Intelligent toilet for men and women,barrier free toilet,toilet
for patients with colostomy,and nursing room are also fully equipped,
hoping to provide more convenient service for everyone
